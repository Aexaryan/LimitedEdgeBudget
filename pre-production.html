PRE-PRODUCTION

FOUNDATION & PLANNING PHASE

Total Budget: $33,000 (8.86% of overall budget)

$33,000

The pre-production phase is crucial for laying the groundwork for a successful reality show. This **$33,000** investment ensures proper planning, talent acquisition, location preparation, and obstacle design before filming begins.

Pre-Production Budget Breakdown

**Set & Obstacle Design** receives the largest allocation at **$12,000** (36.36%), ensuring unique and challenging competition elements. **Travel** and **Marketing Prep** each receive **$7,000** (21.21%) for location scouting and promotional materials.
