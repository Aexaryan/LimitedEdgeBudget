PRODUCTION

FILMING OPERATIONS PHASE

Total Budget: $191,000 (51.26% of overall budget)

$191,000

The production phase represents the core investment in the "Limit Edge" reality show. This **$191,000** allocation ensures high-quality filming operations with professional crew, equipment, and logistics to capture compelling competition footage in Torrevieja, Spain.

Production Budget Breakdown

**Crew Salaries** receive the largest allocation at **$57,000** (29.83%), ensuring skilled technical professionals. **Competitor Fees** and **Host Fees** each receive significant portions to secure top talent, while **Accommodation** and **Equipment** ensure comfortable and professional filming conditions.
