Elite Athletes & Unique Selling Points

Elite Athletes

We seek exceptional individuals who embody the spirit of competition, resilience, and inspiration. Our participants represent the pinnacle of athletic achievement and human potential.

Participant Requirements (Full Version)

Age & Demographics

  • 🎯 Age Range: 20–40 years old
  • Prime stage of physical ability and competitive spirit

Gender Balance

  • ⚖️ 5 men and 5 women
  • Emphasizing gender equality and fair competition

Athletic Background

  • 🏃‍♂️ Minimum 3 years' experience
  • Strength, endurance, or speed sports
  • CrossFit, martial arts, bodybuilding, swimming, etc.

Physical Readiness

  • 💪 Professional health screenings
  • Fitness assessments before joining

Personality Traits

  • 🔥 Fighting spirit and determination
  • 💎 Self-confidence and charisma
  • 🎬 Camera presence and excitement
  • 📖 Inspiring personal story
  • Must resonate with the audience

Media & Growth

  • 📱 Openness to spotlight
  • 👥 Fan engagement during and after show
  • 🚀 Growth potential beyond athletics
  • Transform into inspirational public figures

⭐ Unique Selling Points (USPs) – For Promotion & Sponsorship

🏆 First of Its Kind

First Persian Sports–Endurance Reality Show produced at international standards.

📍 Unique Locations

Dynamic mix of fully equipped 4,000 m² gym complex and natural seaside challenges—delivering both modern athletic intensity and adventurous outdoor visuals.

🎯 Diverse Challenges

Competitions designed to test speed, strength, and endurance across different conditions—making every challenge a unique, cinematic experience.

⚖️ Equal Gender Competition

For the first time, men and women compete under the same conditions, delivering an inspiring message: human ability knows no gender.

🌟 Star Host

Featuring Reza Madadi, a renowned athlete, whose presence adds credibility, energy, and fan appeal.

📖 Storytelling Through Competition

Every contestant carries a life story—personal struggles, motivations, failures, and victories—transformed into powerful narratives through dramatic editing.

🎬 Cinematic Production Quality

Multi-camera coverage, professional cinematography, fast-paced editing, custom-designed music, and modern visual effects ensure a world-class viewing experience.

📱 Cross-Platform Distribution

Dual release strategy through television broadcast + digital platforms/social media, reaching wider and younger audiences.
