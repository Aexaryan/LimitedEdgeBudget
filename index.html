Program Summary

"Limit Edge" is a Persian-language sports reality show featuring 10 male and female athletes, randomly paired in teams, competing daily in endurance, speed, and strength challenges.

With dynamic visuals, emotional depth, and natural environments, it's designed to match international TV standards and captivate Persian-speaking audiences worldwide.

Key Details

  • Language: Persian
  • Contestants: 10 professional/semi-professional athletes (5 men, 5 women)
  • Structure: Daily team challenges + individual point scoring
  • Locations: Torrevieja, Spain - All Star GYM (4000 m² indoor facility) + nature (beach, forest)
  • Episodes: 10 (each shot over one full day)
