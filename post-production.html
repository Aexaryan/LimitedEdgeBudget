POST-PRODUCTION

EDITING & POLISH PHASE

Total Budget: $54,600 (14.65% of overall budget)

$54,600

The post-production phase transforms raw footage into a polished, broadcast-ready reality show. This **$54,600** investment ensures professional editing, sound design, visual effects, and color grading to create an engaging and high-quality final product.

Post-Production Budget Breakdown

**Editing** receives the largest allocation at **$30,000** (54.95%), ensuring comprehensive story development and narrative flow. **Sound Mixing & Music Licensing** and **Color Grading** each receive significant portions to create professional audio and visual quality.
